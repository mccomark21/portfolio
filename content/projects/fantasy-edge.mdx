Fantasy Edge is a browser-based research tool for fantasy baseball managers. It pulls Yahoo Fantasy roster data, blends it with StatCast-derived batter metrics from PyBaseball, Pitcher List SP and RP rankings, and aggregated prospect rankings from MLB Pipeline, FanGraphs, and Prospects Live — all processed entirely client-side with DuckDB WASM. No backend required.
